Weekend Schedule

While we officially tied the knot at Somerville City Hall in February, we're thrilled to celebrate with you all this October. Beyond our pizza party extravaganza, we wanted to share some of our favorite spots with you all: a much-loved brewery in Union Square, the movie theater a short walk from our home, the only decent bagel shop in the neighborhood (Boston does not have good bagels!). We're so happy to share a slice of our life with you all.

Friday October 11

6-8pm: Welcome Drinks

Remnant Brewing
2 Bow Market Way, Somerville, MA 02143

We'll have food (tbd) and an open tab at the front bar. Matt recommends the Dream Pop if they have that on tap.

Saturday October 12

11am-2pm: Lunch at the Movies

Capitol Theater
204 Massachusetts Ave, Arlington, MA 02474

We'll be screening Paddington 2, one of Emily's favorite kid-friendly movies. Come hungry because we will also have a variety of sandwiches from Dave's Fresh Pasta, where we go for lunch on days when we're both working from home.

6pm onward: Pizza Party! (The main event)

Dragon Pizza
233 Elm St, Somerville, MA 02144

Join us for pizza and gelato, alcoholic and nonalcoholic drinks, dancing in “the Dragon's Lair,” some crafts in the kitchen, honestly who knows what else we'll be doing here. Dress to impress, if the person you're impressing is an 8-year-old boy.

Sunday October 13

10am-12pm: Bagels and Beasts

Our house
35 Victoria St #2, Somerville, MA 02144

Come visit our home and meet the two banshees who live with us — our cat, Walter, and our dog, Poppy. We'll have coffee, snacks, and bagels from Goldilox (the only bagel nearby worth knowing).
